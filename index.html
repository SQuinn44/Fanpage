About Lights

Lights Bokan (born Valerie Poxletiner) is a Canadian singer-songwriter and artist. Oscillating between her talents as a musician and a comic artist, she has a dedicated fanbase all around the globe.
Having begun her career at a young age, she currently has eight albums, twenty-six singles, and many collaborations with other musicians. She currently has two albums, "Siberia" and "The Listening", that have been certified Gold in Canada. Her most recent album, "Skin and Earth" has an accompanying comic book series completely written and illustrated by Lights herself. Her most recent albums, "Little Machines" and "Skin and Earth" have both won Best Pop Album of the Year at the Juno Awards.
